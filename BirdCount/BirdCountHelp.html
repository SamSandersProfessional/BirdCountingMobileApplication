Help and FAQs

  
  
  
  
  

  1. Navigate to our registration page in the menu on the top left of your screen. Press on the three horizontal lines to open the menu.

  2. Click 'My Account' to navigate to the registration screen.

  3. Click Login if you have made an account already. If you don't have a user account, then click on the sign-up link to make a new account.

  4. Then, add your user details. You may select 'forgot your password?' to reset your password if you can't remember it.
